# ![Banner for Mo' Enchants: Adds more enchantments and curses!](https://i.imgur.com/GAFP3Vc.png)

> [!NOTE]
> Mo' Enchants is a **Forge** mod. There are no plans to support other mod loaders.

Mo' Enchants is a load of enchantments and curses, added into Minecraft while still keeping a somewhat vanilla feel.

[<img alt="Works with Enchantment Descriptions" height="96" src="https://i.imgur.com/nBaAh0P.png"/>](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/enchantment-descriptions)

### Enchantments & Curses

| Enchantment/Curse Name | Max Level | Tool(s)          | Description                                                                                                  |
|------------------------|-----------|------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Angel's Blessing       | III       | Totem of Undying | Gives your Totem of Undying extra uses, +1 use per every level.                                              |
| Aqua Slash             | IV        | Weapons          | Does more damage to mobs with a water weakness (Blazes, Endermen, Magma Cubes)                               |
| Bloodthirst            | II        | Weapons          | When right-clicked with this enchantment, take 2.5 hearts in exchange for Strength and Speed for 12 seconds. |
| Conduction             | I         | Bows             | If a lightning storm is happening when you shoot a mob, the mob gets struck with lightning.                  |
| Critical Blow          | III       | Weapons          | Allows for the chance (10% x level) of critical hits, which do 150% damage.                                  |
| Curse of Aquaphobia    | I         | Armor            | Makes you take half a heart of damage every half-second.                                                     |
| Curse of Insomnia      | I         | Helmets          | Die of a bad dream, whenever you try to sleep.                                                               |
| Curse of Boiling       | I         | Armor            | When you are in a hot/dry biome or in the nether, you get set on fire.                                       |
| Curse of Density       | I         | Armor            | Makes you move 15% slower.                                                                                   |
| Curse of Frostbite     | I         | Armor            | When you are in a freezing biome, your speed is lowered by 30%.                                              |
| Curse of Harming       | I         | Weapons          | Damages you when you damage any other mob.                                                                   |
| Curse of Sacrifice     | IV        | Weapons          | When you attack a mob, it drains some of your lifeforce, and gives it to the attacked mob.                   |
| Curse of Scorching     | I         | Armor            | When you get hit, you get ignited on fire.                                                                   |
| Detonation             | I         | Bows             | When you kill a mob, create an explosion that damages everything around the killed mob except blocks.        |
| Fireproofing           | I         | Chestplates      | Wearing a chestplate with this enchantment makes you immune to fire and lava.                                |
| First Strike           | I         | Weapons          | Does 25% more damage if the hit is the first strike.                                                         |
| Frost                  | III       | Bows             | Freezes mobs, which slows the mob by 15% \* level.                                                           |
| Green Thumb            | I         | Hoes             | Prevents you from mining crops that aren't fully grown, and stems.                                           |
| Hunter                 | IV        | Weapons          | Does more damage to animals.                                                                                 |
| Ice Aspect             | III       | Weapons          | Freezes mobs, which slows the mob by 15% \* level.                                                           |
| Levitating             | III       | Bows             | Shooting mobs with this enchantment makes them fly.                                                          |

| Magma Walker           | I         | Boots            | Allows you to walk on lava, simular to Frost Walker.                                                         |

| Rock Mending           | III       | Tools            | Has a chance (30%, 60%, 80%) of not damaging the tool when mining any sort of rock.                          |
| Saving Grace           | I         | Boots            | When you fall into the void, get launched back up.                                                           |
| Smelting Touch         | I         | Tools            | Automatically smelts any block you mine.                                                                     |
| Swiftness              | III       | Boots            | Makes you able to run 20% faster, per level.                                                                 |
| Vitality               | IV        | Armor            | Adds half a heart to your max health for every level, up to IV.                                              |

| Ultimate Finish        | I         | Weapons          | When you kill a mob, create an explosion that damages everything around you except blocks and you.           |
| Weightless             | II        | Leggings         | Allows you to jump higher, the level of the enchantment determines how high you jump.                        |
